About me:

After a decade of development in a corporate enterprise setting, I've branched out on my own. Over the years I have gathered a toolset of different languages and platforms such as Python or Autohotkey, JavaScript, PHP, C# or VB.Net, SQL and noSQL database integrations, and several resources to tap to fill in just about everything else.

I have created enterprise-wide applications for productivity tracking and database connectivity, suites of assistant tools based on the user requirements, real-time analytics, and standalone processing bots that have saved millions of dollars in freed up employee time.

I believe in saving time and effort. Robots can do a lot, and do it fast, but we are possible of much more. Our time is valuable, why spend it doing something we don't have to?
