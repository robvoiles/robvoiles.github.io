About me:

I have worked in coding and automation for 8 years, having tackled a variety of projects. Over the years I have gathered a toolset of prototyping languages like Python or Autohotkey and class-based languages such as C# or VB.Net as well as RPA platforms like BluePrism or UIPath.

I have created enterprise-wide applications for productivity tracking and database connectivity, suites of assistant tools based on the users' requirements, and standalone processing bots to save millions of dollars in freed up employee time.

I believe in saving time and effort. Robots can do a lot, and do it fast, but we are possible of much more - if we can open up the time for us to do it.
